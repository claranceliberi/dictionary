INKORANYAMUGA Y'IKORANABUHANGA

# Ijambo ry'ibanze

Inteko y'Umuco ifite inshingano yo gukungahaza no guteza imbere Ikinyarwanda mu ngeri z'ubumenyi zitandukanye. Bumwe mu buryo bukoreshwa kugira ngo iyo nshingano yuzuzwe ni ukwandika inkoranyamagambo n'inkoranyamuga.

Inteko y'Umuco ifite gahunda irambuye yo kwandika inkoranyamuga zinoze zo mu ngeri zose z'ubumenyi kugira ngo irusheho gushakira Ikinyarwanda amagambo mashya gikeneye, yungura ubumenyi bw'Abanyarwanda kandi inabafasha kurushaho gusabana mu bumenyi baniteza imbere.

Mu gukungahaza Ikinyarwanda ku muga y'ubuhanga n'ubumenyi, Inteko y'Umuco yanditse Inkoranyamuga y'Ikoranabuhanga igira ngo ikemure ikibazo cyari kimaze iminsi usanga abafatanyabikorwa bagaragaza ko hakenewe inkoranya ivuga ku ikoranabuhanga mu Kinyarwanda.

Mu kwandika inkoranyamuga y'ikoranabuhanga mu Kinyarwanda hakurikijwe igenamikorere ry'ubushakashatsi mu by'ihangamuga, hakorwa ikusanyamuga haherewe ku magambo akoreshwa mu bigo bifite aho bihuriye n'ikoranabuhanga, twavuga nka Minisiteri y'Ikoranabuhanga na Inovasiyo (MINICT), Ikigo cy'u Rwanda Gishinzwe Ikoranabuhanga mu Itumanaho n'Isakazabumenyi (RISA) n'Urwego Ngenzuramikorere (RURA). Inteko y'Umuco yakusanyije kandi amuga mu bigo bikoresha cyane ikoranabuhanga, ibyaduhaye amuga bikoresha muri serivisi batanga. Hasuwe kandi imbuga zitandukanye za murandasi zibonekaho intonde z'amuga y'ikoranabuhanga mu zindi ndimi. Hibanzwe by'umwihariko ku muga Umunyarwanda usanzwe azakenera mu gukoresha mudasobwa n'ibijyanye na yo.

Iyi nkoranyamuga y'ikoranabuhanga mu Kinyarwanda yanditswe ku bufatanye bw'inzego. Abakozi b'Inteko y'Umuco bagize uruhare mu iyandikwa ry'iyi nkoranyamuga y'ikoranabuhanga mu Kinyarwanda ni KABAGEMA Egide, MUSABEYEZU Théogène, MBARUBUKEYE Jean de la Croix, UWERA Marcelline na MUSHIKIWASE Chantal. Nyuma yo gutegura inyandiko y'ibanze y'inkoranyamuga y'ikoranabuhanga, Inteko y'Umuco yegereye abafatanyabikorwa bayo bayiha inzobere zo kunoza iyo nyandiko.
