INKORANYAMUGA YIKORANABUHANGA

Uburyo bwo guhanga no kwinjiza inyandiko ku rubuga rwa murandasi, bityo ibihashyizwe bikitwa inyandiko yo ku rubuga.

**Ihangashusho** (ihaangashusho). HI: *Ihangashusho rya mudasobwa* (ihaangashusho ryaa mūdasobwā). Eng: *Computer imaging*. Fr: *Imagerie informatique*. NK: *Ikoranabuhanga rya mudasobwa*. SH: *Gufata amashusho kuri mudasobwa bikubiyemo gufata ishusho ya sisitemu y'imikorere, cyangwa ishusho y'icyitegererezo, uhereye kuri mudasobwa y'icyitegererezo ukayishyira mu gikoresho kimwe cyangwa byinshi.*

**Ihange nyamakuru** (ihaāngge nyamākurū). Eng: *Information theory*. Fr: *Théorie de l'information*. NK: *Ikoranabuhanga rya mudasobwa*. SH: *Hakoreshejwe isesengura ry’amakuru, hashakwa umubare w’imyanya koranabuhanga wa ngombwa mu guhagararira amakuru agize ukwiyumvisha amakuru ava ku isoko.*

**Ihange nyamwikoresha** (ihaāngge nyamwiikoreesha). Eng: *Automata Theory*. Fr: *Théorie des automates*. NK: *Ikoranabuhanga rya mudasobwa*. SH: *Ishami ryibanda ku ihange ry’ikoranabuhanga rishingiye ku ibonezabitekererezo ry’imibare, rigatanga ishingiro ry’iyumvwa ry’imibare no kugena za sisitemu z’indundo na musesenguzi, rikiga ku mashini nyabuhanga zitwa nyamwikoresha no ku bushobozi bwazo bwo kubara.*

**Ihange ry’ibara** (ihaāngge ry’ibara). Eng: *Theory of computation*. Fr: *Théorie du calcul*. NK: *Ikoranabuhanga rya mudasobwa*. SH: *Ishami rihuza siyansi ya mudasobwa n'imibare rivuga ku buryo ibibazo bishobora gukemurwa hakoreshejwe uburyo bwo kubara, hakoreshejwe uburyo bwa aligoritime.*

**Ihange ry’ibarika** (ihaāngge ry’ibarika). Eng: *Computability Theory; Computability Theory*. Fr: *Théorie de la calculabilité*. NK: *Ikoranabuhanga rya mudasobwa*. SH: *Ubuhanga bwo kubara bushakashaka ibibazo bishobora gukemurwa n’imbonezamibare na za mudasobwa byibanda ku mbibi z’ibara no kuba hariho ibibazo bidashobora kubonerwa igisubizo twavuga nk’ikibazo cy’ihagarara ry’igikoresho koranabuhanga.*

**Ihaza koranabuhanga** (ihaaza kōranabūhaānga). Eng: *IT coverage; Coverage*. Fr: *Couverture informatique*. NK: *Ikoranabuhanga rya*
