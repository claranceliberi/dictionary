INKORANYAMUGA Y'IKORANABUHANGA

Urusobe ntangamakuru. SH: Urwungano rugenga amakuru ari ku gikoresho gishyizwe muri mudasobwa.

Ingengamenyesha (ingeengamenyeesha). Eng: Notification Manager. Fr: Gestionnaire de notifications. NK: Ikoranabuhanga rya mudasobwa. SH: Serivisi ya sisitemu ishinzwe gucunga no kwerekana amamenyesha ava kuri porogaramu.

Ingenzuramikorere (igēenzuuramīkorēre) HI: Sisitemu y'ubugenzunzi (siisiteēmu y'ūbugenzunzi). Eng: Monitoring System. Fr: Système de surveillance. NK: Ikoranabuhanga rya mudasobwa. SH: Sisitemu ikurikirana uko urubuga cyangwa sisitemu y'amakuru ikora no kumenya ibibazo by'umutekano.

Ingenzuramivugire (ingēenzuuramīvugīre). Eng: Spell Checker. Fr: Correcteur orthographique. NK: Ikoranabuhanga rya mudasobwa. SH: Porogaramu ifasha gusuzuma no gukosora amakosa y'imyandikire mu nyandiko.

Ingenzuramutungo koranabuhanga (ingēenzuuramūtuungo kōranabūhaānga). Eng: Provisioning Manager. Fr: Gestionnaire de provisionnement. NK: Ikoranabuhanga rya mudasobwa. SH: Igice cya mudasobwa gitanga ubushobozi kigenzura umutima wa mudasobwa kigakora ibisabwe haba ari ugutanga uburenganzira cyangwa kubukuraho bishingiye ku mabwiriza yatanzwe n'uburyo umutima wa mudasobwa uteguye.
